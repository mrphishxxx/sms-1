This white paper represents my current research into how sms companies are using APIs. My goal is to look at all of the sms APIs available, identify the best of them, and document what the characteristics of these providers are.

As with all of my white papers I will profile each of the leading providers, and identify the common building blocks across them, in hopes of establishing a blueprint of each provider, as well as aggregate industry blueprint across all the leading providers.
